Chocolate Lava Cake

A rich, gooey chocolate dessert that melts in your mouth.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 oz dark chocolate
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). 2. Melt butter and chocolate together. 3. Whisk in eggs and sugar. 4. Stir in flour until smooth. 5. Pour into ramekins and bake 12 mins. 6. Serve immediately with ice cream.
